Hwoarang Guide

Introduction:

Welcome to my guide for Hwoarang! This character is very complex so make some time to learn him. He can be really hard to wrap your head around and is really different from the regular Tekken characaters.

This guide will be divded into several sections; each covering different aspects of his gameplay.

Key Moves:

This section is composed of even more sections going off the Japanese Concept of Sansukumi:

Mixup (Low/Mid):

    Low:
  • d3,4 rfs or rff d3,4
  • db4(~f)
  • lfs db4
  • rfs db4,3(,4)
  • db3
    Mid:
  • 3~4 and rfs 3~4
  • JFSR
  • lff u4
  • lfs f3_rfs b3
  • lf4 uf4_rfs uf4
  • rff b2
  • rff df3

Keepout:

  • df2
  • Random ass b3
  • df4
  • H. ch 2,4 rfs f4,4
  • H. ch rff df4~f rfs f4,4

Whiff Punish:

  • b3
  • df2
  • 4,3
  • 1,2
Heat Mechanic:

He has the following moves as Heat Engagers:

  • df3+4
  • ff4
  • rff df3
  • lfs f3
  • rfs b3

He has several enhanced moves in Heat State:

  • H. rfs db4,3,4 (nc)
  • H. rfs f4,4 (nc, ncc when combined with other moves)
Punishment:

Hwoarang's punishment is quite decent:

    Standing:
  • i10: 1,2(,3_4)
  • i11: 4,3
  • i13: df3+4
  • i15: df2
  • i16: b3
    While Standing:
  • i10: fc1
  • i11: ws4,4
  • i14: ws3
  • i15: ws2,3
  • i16 or more: cc b3
Flowchart:

His flowchart? d3,4 all day!

Combo & Combo Adjustments:

His combo is pretty easy but highly configurable, which is a part of the fun playing him:

  • f4 rfs 3~4 uf3+4~f lfs uf4 T!
  • db4~f rfs 3~4 uf3+4~f lfs uf4 T!
  • T! f4 rfs 3~4 uf3+4~fx3
